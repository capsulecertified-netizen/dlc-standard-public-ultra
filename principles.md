# Principles (Ultra‑Abstract)

1) **Familiarity first** — A Capsule behaves like a normal file.  
2) **Truth travels** — Essential authorship, origin, and context remain attached as the file moves.  
3) **Lightweight by default** — Provide clarity without heavy infrastructure.  
4) **Open posture** — Designed to fit into diverse tools and platforms.  
5) **Incremental adoption** — Support grows from simple display to deeper awareness, at the adopter’s pace.
